04 — Narrative Case Studies

Where story and mechanic share a spine

Two solo narrative-design projects, each built out as a full case study: premise, worldbuilding, systems that carry the story, and a writing sample.

Serpent & Shravan

Case Study
Narrative-Driven / Interactive Story Mobile · PC (Unreal Engine) Narrative Designer (Solo)

A child listens to an elder recount sacred stories of the Madhushravani festival — and as the stories unfold, the line between myth and reality begins to blur. Told through dual layers, a grounded rural household during monsoon season, and a mythological layer the player steps into, the piece explores innocence versus understanding, faith and tradition, and oral storytelling as legacy. A dialogue-pause and story-playback system carries the loop: narration, play, question, dialogue, and resolution — with visual direction pulled from Madhubani art.

"They say the serpent does not strike without reason… but reason is not always meant for us to understand."

Magma Chronicle

Case Study
Story-Driven Action Adventure Mobile Narrative Designer / Worldbuilder

In the volcanic world of Eridia, five flame-wielding heroes rise against an ancient entity — the Firelord — whose awakening threatens to consume everything. A mythic narrative about power, sacrifice, and rebirth, told through environmental storytelling and combat-driven progression across an early–mid–late arc, with elemental combat and character-switching mechanics built to carry the story's themes into the moment-to-moment gameplay.

"Fire does not choose what it consumes… only what remains after."
